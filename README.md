# 📊 Task 4: Data Storytelling & Statistical Validation

## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on transforming sales data into meaningful business insights using data storytelling and statistical validation techniques. The goal is to analyze trends, validate assumptions, and provide actionable recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Convert raw data into a clear business narrative
* Identify key trends and performance drivers
* Apply hypothesis-based validation
* Present insights through a structured presentation

---

## 🧠 Key Analysis Performed

* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
* Sales trend analysis (time-based)
* Product line and country-wise performance
* Customer-level insights
* Deep-dive analysis on high-performing segments

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

* Total Sales, Order Count, Avg Order Value (KPIs)
* Sales by Country
* Sales by Product Line
* Monthly Sales Trends
* Top Customers by Revenue
* Interactive Filters (Year, Product Line)

---

## 🔍 Hypothesis Testing

**Hypothesis:**
High revenue is driven by specific product lines and regions.

**Validation:**
Analysis showed that certain segments (e.g., Classic Cars and USA region) contribute significantly to total sales.

**Conclusion:**
The hypothesis is supported by data insights.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Classic Cars is the top-performing product line
* USA generates the highest revenue
* Sales show seasonal trends across months
* Revenue is concentrated among a few high-value customers

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing segments
* Optimize inventory for peak sales periods
* Strengthen relationships with top customers
* Improve strategies for low-performing segments

---
